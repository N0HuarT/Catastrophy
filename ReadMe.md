# **The 7 Catalysts --- Core Identity**

- 1 *Active Catalyst* (equipped / Attuned)
- 2 *Bound Catalysts* (passive AI units)
- You issue **commands**, not micromanage
- **1 Active Catalyst** â†’ modifies weapon + grants Signature Spell

- Command one of the 2 Catalyst to cast its spell (Q/E/1/2)
- Equipped Catalyst = Attunement
  Each Catalyst is defined by:
- **Primary Gain** --- what players actively want
- **Secondary Effects** --- supporting stats (HP, CDR, accuracy, etc.)
- **Distortion / Penalty** --- why pure builds are dangerous
- **Design Role** --- the gameplay fantasy it fulfills
