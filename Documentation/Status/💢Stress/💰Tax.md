**50–74% Stress:**  
Taking damage causes you to drop **small resource/pots pickups**.
**75–99% Stress:**  
Dropped pickups are **larger and attract enemies**; enemies that touch them gain a 10% damage buff for 10 sec.
**100% Stress — HOARDER’S PANIC:**
- Enter **Panicked Hoarding** for 5 seconds:
    - All nearby loot is violently pulled to you (including from containers, enemies).        
    - You gain +100% armor but **move 60% slower**.
    - Taking damage causes you to **explode loot**, damaging you and nearby enemies.
- After 6 sec, forcibly swap; Greed is **locked** for 30 sec.
_Dropped Loots last 20 seconds_
