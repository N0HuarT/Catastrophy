- **Glaive Summoner**  
    Summon a stationary glaive/tower that attacks enemies automatically.  
    **Hold**: longer charge → higher durability, faster attack speed, increased damage; mana scales with duration.
- **Tornado**  
    Creates a wind vortex that knocks up enemies in its area.  
    **Hold**: longer charge → larger AoE, stronger knock-up, more damage; mana scales with duration.
- **Thundercloud**  
    Strikes enemies in an area with lightning bolts.  
    **Hold**: longer channel → faster strike rate, more targets hit; mana scales with duration.
- **Firewall**  
    Creates a wall of fire that burns enemies and may scare them into walking on surfaces.  
    **Hold**: longer charge → larger/longer wall, higher burn damage, extended duration; mana scales with duration.
- **Ice / Frost Zone**  
    Slows enemies and has a chance to frostbite.  
    **Hold**: longer charge → larger zone; frostbitten enemies can be shattered for bonus damage/resources; mana scales with duration
- **Shadow Vortex**  
	Creates a blackhole that pulls enemies toward its center, disrupting positioning.  
	**Hold**: longer charge → stronger pull, larger radius, longer duration; mana scales with duration
- **Saturating Wave**  
    Sends a flowing wave that damages enemies and restores mana/charge for yourself or nearby Catalysts.  
    **Hold**: longer charge → wider area, stronger sustain effects, slows enemies or creates lingering water zones; mana scales with duration.
